Hello

I am Mazharul


CV | Github | Google Scholar | Twitter | LinkedIn


I am a Ph.D. student at UW-Madison focusing on applied cryptography with a broad research interest in solving security and privacy problems related to user authentication and machine learning. So far my works have focused on designing privacy-preserving ways to record users' login behavior and protecting users against ``advanced online guessing attacks" using cryptographic techniques. I am also working on blending traditional cryptography with deep neural networks (DNN) to allow learning over encrypted datasets.


Take some time to visit the list of my publications and research projects page to see the exciting projects I am working on!
