Hello

I am Mazharul


CV | Github | Google Scholar | Twitter | LinkedIn


I am an applied cryptographer with a board research interest in solving security, privacy problems related to user authentication and machine learning. So far my works have focused on designing privacy preserving way to record users' login behaviour and protecting users against ``advanced online guessing attacks" using cryptographic techniques. I am also working on blending traditional cryptography with deep neural networks (DNN) to allow training and prediction over encrypted datasets.

Please take some time to visit the list of my publications and research projects page to see the exciting projects I am working on!
